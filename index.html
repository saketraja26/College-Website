Courses We Offer

Graduate

Bachelor of Science (Computer Science) is Three Year and BSc Computer Science(Hons) is Four years full time program designed to develop student’s knowledge and expertise in Computer Science to be competent for industry exposure. Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) is a 3 year and BCA(Hons.) is a 4 year Full-Time undergraduate program in computer applications which is designed to meet the skilled human resource in computer field.

Post graduate

This program is application-oriented to meet the shortage of skilled human resource. The program focuses on basic computer science, programming languages, databases, management, web technologies, data science, mobile development and advanced application development. Master of Computer Application is a two-year Full-Time Program. MCA program is a blend of Computer Science, Management and Engineering courses.

Phd

Ph.D. is a 3 years full-time study Program which provides a rewarding career in Computer Science. The Ph.D. program in Computer Science is offered to students who are interested in advanced learning and research in all domain of Computer Science. A student should be able to find the solution for the problems faced by society and different domains of technology in computer science and other fields.
